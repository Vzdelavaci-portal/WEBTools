# 🎨 Palette Types

## Monochromatic

Different shades and tints of the same color.

Example:

```text
Light Blue
Blue
Primary Blue
Dark Blue
Very Dark Blue
```

Perfect for:

- dashboards
- business websites
- minimalist UI

---

## Complementary

Uses opposite colors from the color wheel.

Example:

```text
Blue ↔ Orange
Purple ↔ Yellow
Red ↔ Cyan
```

Perfect for:

- call-to-action buttons
- marketing pages
- high contrast designs

---

## Analogous

Uses neighboring colors.

Example:

```text
Blue
Blue-Cyan
Cyan
Teal
Green
```

Perfect for:

- modern interfaces
- harmonious designs
- creative websites

---

## Triadic

Three evenly spaced colors on the color wheel.

Example:

```text
Blue
Red
Yellow
```

Perfect for:

- colorful projects
- illustrations
- creative branding

---

## Split Complementary

A softer alternative to complementary palettes.

Example:

```text
Blue
Red-Orange
Yellow-Orange
```

Perfect for:

- balanced designs
- modern landing pages
- SaaS products
